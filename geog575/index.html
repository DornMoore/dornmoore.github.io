What are the Biggest Drivers of CO2 Pollution

Recent reports from the IPCC (Inter-governmental Panel on Climate Change) have highlighted the impending grave impacts on global weather and ecosystem health. The October 7, 2018, New York Times article by Coral Davenport, Major Climate Report Describes a Strong Risk of Crisis as Early as 2040, summarizes the findings of the IPCC report. To put these facts into context, it's useful to explore the past and how we arrived at the current state of affairs.

Although the drivers of climate change and, more specifically CO2 (carbon dioxide) emissions, are diverse and complex, population is seen as one of the primary culprits. Although the three most populous countries have consistently been the biggest CO2 polluters, there is still significant complexity to the topic.

Who am I?

I've been a geographer with a focus on using geospatial tools for environmental conservation around the world for 20 years. The majority of that time, I've worked with the International Crane Foundation to ensure a future for the world's 15 species of cranes.

One of the greatest challenges, and rewards, of a carreer in a technical field is the need to continue learning throughout your life. I am constantly amazed at the changes over the past 20 years. I enjoy new challenges and helping people and orgianzations make a positive difference in the world through technology.

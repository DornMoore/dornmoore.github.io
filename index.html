Who am I?

I've been a geographer with a focus on using geospatial tools for environmental conservation around the world for 20 years. The majority of that time, I've worked with the International Crane Foundation to ensure a future for the world's 15 species of cranes.

I enjoy new challenges and helping people and organizations make a positive difference in the world through technology. I am happy when I'm able to make a technical problem easier for one of my colleagues or use cartographic tools to help make a difference in the world.
